Overview

Description

The Beast Temple is a large, untaimed wilderness, filled with dense forests, winding rivers, and towering mountains. It is a place where nature reigns supreme, and venturing inside is both thrilling and dangerous.

There is something unnerving about the place, mysterious stones and pillars are scattered throughout the land, covered in strange carvings and symbols. Blood stains the ground in certain areas, and the occasional bones of unfortunate travellers can be found. It is a land of danger, and sacrifices must be made in order to treck through.

Inhabitants

Almost nobody lives here, apart from a few hermits and hunters who have chosen to make their homes in the wilderness. The vast majority of the inhabitants are wild animals, ranging from small Vermins, Insects and Fish, to larger Canine, Hooved or Avian creatures. Reptiles and Amphibians can also be found in certain areas, as well as a few Cryptids.

Hierarchy

The Beast Temple is ruled by Leshy, a powerful Scrybe who has a deep connection with nature. He sees beasts as the noblest form of life, and believes that they should be respected and protected. Despite this, he still has his henchmen which he relies on to keep the temple in order and to challenge any intruders.

These henchmen all exploit the resources of the land in different ways. The Prospector searches for gold, while The Angler fishes the rivers and lakes for food. The Lumberjack harvests the forests for wood, and The Trapper hunts the wild animals for fur and meat, trading some of it with outsiders.

Progression

There is a clear, set path through the temple that the player must follow in order to reach Leshy's cabin. However, this path is not without its challenges, as the player must face and defeat each of Leshy's henchmen in order to progress.

Each area has its own challenges and way of being traversed, both shaped by the environment and the exploitation of its resources. The player must adapt to each area, using different strategies and tactics to overcome the obstacles in their way.

The henchmen will all be found at the end of their respective areas, and will challenge the player as instructed by Leshy. If the player manages to defeat them, they will be able to proceed to the next area. Once all four henchmen have been defeated, the player can finally reach Leshy's cabin and face him in a final showdown.
