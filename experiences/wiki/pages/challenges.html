This page is still under heavy development.

Challenges

Upon starting a run, the Player may choose to turn on additional Challenges to make their run harder. There is currently no benefit to doing this, though it could have to do with Talkers or Lost Journal Logs.

Cloverless

Removes the Clover from the game, making it impossible to reroll.
Snuffed Out

You only get 1 candle, making every death fatal by default.
Constrained

Every card will have its cost changed to the main resource of the starting Temple.
Lycanthropy

Scrybes get an extra Moon phase. This phase is meant to be almost impossible.
One shot

The player doesn't get their discard back after finishing the first phase of a boss.
Purity

Cards can now only recieve one modification, the Player has to make it count.
Smaller Scales

Battles end when the scales reach either candle, making them much shorter but also much more dangerous.
Out of Stock

Every item can only be picked up once. This resets once all items have been picked up once.
Frenzy

Every card, both the Player's and the Enemies' have their sigils upgraded by default.
Undecisive

Most Sigils that normally give the player a choice will now decide randomly instead.
Weak Start

Every card in the starter deck gets a negative sigil depending on the Temple:

  1. Beast: Familiar
  2. Undead: Boneless
  3. Tech: Power Leak
  4. Magick: Gem Dependent
