Events

Note: If you want to check out the battle events, see Battles.

Here are listed all the different nodes that exist to help you build your deck. They can be found on the map by exploring the different areas.

The Common Events can be found everywhere relatively frequently, but the other Temple Events are locked in their respective temples. On top of that, each Temple Event has an Area of Origin, which must be reached before the event can appear. It will also appear there more than anywhere else.

Common Events

These events are present in all temples, and are the backbone of deck building.
IconNameDescriptionType▼
Tinkering

Rebecha accompanies the Player to the next event, tinkering it and buffing its effect.

This event can only appear once per Area, and therefore has no Tinker effect.

 Event Buff
Card Choice

The player gets a choice of 3 cards from the current temple.

Every card has a 1/6 chance of being an Uncommon, otherwise it's a Common.

Tinker effect - Cards now have a 1/2 chance of being Uncommon.

 Card Gain
Wandering Trader

The player can trade one of their cards for a new one from any temple.

They get to choose between a card of the same Role, a card of the same Cost or a card of the same Temple. If the card is rarer than Common, it will give a card of the same Rarity instead of Temple.

If none of the cards please them, they may take their card back and leave.

Tinker effect - The player can choose what characteristic of their card (Role, Cost or Temple) they want back, and they will be given a choice of 3 cards with that characteristic instead.

 Card Gain
Booster Pack

A random temple is selected, and 3 cards are drawn just like the Card Draw event.

Two additional common cards are drawn from the other 3 temples, for a total of 5 cards from which the player must pick 2.

Tinker effect - The player may choose the first Temple, and all cards have a 1/6 chance of being Uncommon.

 Card Gain
Cost Choice

The player is given a choice of 3 costs based on the 2 most common costs in their deck. They are then given a choice of 2 common cards from any temple with that cost.

Tinker effect - The Player can choose the cost they want, and get a choice of 3 cards of that cost.

 Card Gain
Role Choice

The player is given a choice of 3 card roles. They are then given a choice of 2 common cards from any temple with that role.

Tinker effect - The Player can choose the role they want, and get a choice of 3 cards of that role.

 Card Gain
Recycle

The player may destroy one of their cards in exchange for Foils.

Common cards are worth 3 foils, Uncommons are worth 5 and Rares are worth 8. The card also gives 2 extra foils per modification it has.

Tinker effect - The player gains double the foils they normally would.

 Foils Gain
Moon Altar

The player destroys a card in their deck to get a choice of 3 random Boons.

If they are already carrying 3 boons, they replace one, or choose not to interact with the event at all.

Tinker effect - The player gets a single Major Boon instead.

 Boon Gain
Item Pack

The player gets a choice of 3 random Items.

If they are already carrying 3 items, they may replace one, or choose not to.

Tinker effect - The player gets as many item choices as it takes to fill all their slots.

 Item Gain
Enchantment

The player may select a card to infuse with a random Essence.

If they select a card that already has an Essence, they may deplete their health by 1 to give it a Prism Essence.

Tinker effect - Instead of an Essence, the card gains a permanent Gem Sigil.

 Card Buff
Trader Shop

The Trader shop sells different types of goods in exchange for Foils:

  • A Booster Pack from a temple of the player's choice
  • A random Item
  • A random event exclusive to the current temple
  • A random common card from any temple (x3)

Tinker effect - Every good restocks infinitely.

Beast Temple Events

These events are present in the Beast Temple, and are placed in order of appearance.
IconNameDescriptionPriceType▼
Hunting Grounds

The Player sends one of their cards to go hunting. They can make the hunt last from 1-3 battles (may not exceed the number of battles in the area), after which the card comes back from the hunt.

Whn the card comes back, the player gets to roll 1 success die per battle the hunt took to complete. Outcomes are : +1 power, +1 health or +2 health.

Apart from a 1-battle hunt, before rolling success dice, risk coins are flipped, one per battle the hunt took to complete, minus one. 1 failed risk coin flip removes 1 dice roll, 2 destroys the card.

Tinker effect - The Player may choose the outcome of success dies.

 3 foils Card Buff
Taxidermy

3 pelts are presented to the player out of a pool of 6. Each Pelt has a Tribe and a pool of sigils from which 1 is chosen. The Player must select one pelt to graft to a card of their choice.

If the chosen card shares a tribe with the pelt, then the sigil on the pelt is added to the card normally. If not, the sigil is added with a Tribal Conditional and the card gains the Tribe of the Pelt.

Tinker effect - The Player gets a choice between all 6 pelts.

 5 foils Card Buff

Undead Temple Events

These events are present in the Undead Temple, and are placed in order of appearance.
IconNameDescriptionPriceType▼
Runestone

When the Player arrives at this event, a random Rune is drawn from 8 possible runes. The Player must choose a card to recieve one of the following effects depending on the rune :

  • Uruz (ᚢ) : The card gains 2 health.
  • Thurisaz (ᚦ) : The card gains 1 power.
  • Fehu (ᚠ) : The card gains a "reward" sigil.
  • Othala (ᛟ) : The card gains the Haunter sigil.
  • Gebo (ᚷ) : The card gets a random sigil replaced with another.
  • Perthro (ᛈ) : A coin is flipped, and depending on the outcome:
    Heads: The card gains 2 power.
    Tails: The card is destroyed.
  • Nauthiz (ᚾ) : The card loses 1 power.
  • Hagalaz (ᚺ) : The card loses a random sigil.

Tinker effect - The Player may choose the Rune they want instead.

 3 foils Card Buff
Boneyard

The player may choose a card to halve the cost of, rounded down. Doing so will give the card the Brittle sigil.

If the card already has the sigil, then the cost will be doubled instead, and Brittle will be removed.

Tinker effect - The card does not gain Brittle when halving its cost, and does not double its cost when removing the sigil.

 4 foils Cost Change
Seance

The Player must choose two cards, one of which will gain the Ensouled sigil and the other being destroyed, possessing the first card.

If the Player selects a card that already has Ensouled, it will simply be transferred from one card to another. If both cards have the sigil, then their possessors swap hosts.

Tinker effect - The card doesn't get destroyed when possessing another.

 6 foils Card Buff
Treasure

The Player has to choose between 5 random, face-down chests. Each chest is given a phrase of description to help the player make their choice. The different hints and rewards can be found in the table below.

Tinker effect - The Player may open two chests instead of one.

Technology Temple Events

These events are present in the Technology Temple, and are placed in order of appearance.
IconNameDescriptionPriceType▼
Gemification

The Player is given 3 colors of Gemification, an Orange, Green and Blue one. They may distribute these however they wish: all to one card, or spread across multiple cards.

When the gemification color is provided in battle:

  • Orange : The card gains +1 Power.
  • Green : The card gains +2 Health.
  • Blue : The card's cost is reduced. (-1 Blood/Gem | -2 Energy/Bones)

If a Prism Gem is provided in battle, the Player may choose which buff is active during their turn for any Gemified card.

Tinker effect - An additional Prism Gemification is given to the player, that is able to provide a buff of the Player's choice (among the three regular buffs) but with any color.

 5 foils Card Buff

Magick Temple Events

These events are present in the Magick Temple, and are placed in order of appearance.
IconNameDescriptionPriceType▼
Potion Shop

The Player is given 3 potions to purchase, out of a total pool of 8. Potions are special cards that permanently give sigils to other cards in battle:

  • Bloated Potion (6 foils) : Blood Pact
  • Clarity Potion (7 foils) : Prism Gem
  • Confusion Potion (4 foils) : Rampager
  • Fire Potion (2 foils) : Burning
  • Health Potion (4 foils) : Convalesce
  • Levitation Potion (6 foils) : Airborne
  • Stench Potion (5 foils) : Stinky
  • Unstable Potion (3 foils) : Detonator

Tinker effect - The Player is offered one potion for free.

 2 foils Card Buff
Painting

The Player may change the color of a gem cost in one of their cards, the color of a gem provided by one of their cards, or the color of an essence.

If they don't have any of these three, then the event simply turns into the Enchantment event.

Tinker effect - Instead of changing the color to another, the Player can change it to a prism instead.

 4 foils Cost Change
Mirror

The Player may select a card to warp in 3 different mirrors. Each mirror rolls a random buff and debuff that will be applied to the card. The player then picks one version to replace his current one. Buffs are :

  • +1 Power
  • +1 Health
  • +2 Health
  • Gain a random Sigil
  • Cost -1 Blood/Gem | -2 Energy/Bones
  • Change the cost type

Debuffs are :

  • -1 Power
  • -1 Health
  • Lose a random Sigil
  • Random Sigil becomes Arcane
  • Cost +1 Blood/Gem | +2 Energy/Bones
  • Nothing

Tinker effect - Instead of choosing between three mirrors, the Player chooses what buff they get. The debuff is still random.

 4 foils Varied
Ritual

The Player may destroy a card to upgrade the sigil of another card. Sigils that have been grafted cannot be upgraded.

Tinker effect - Instead of destroying the card, the Player only has to remove a sigil from a card.

 6 foils Sigil Buff
