Overview

Description

The Undead Temple occupies the largest island, strangely shaped like a large skeletal hand. The whole island is like a gigantic Graveyard, where the dead are brought back to life in order to die again in an endless cycle.

Inhabitants

Zombies, Skeletons and Ghosts are plentiful here, with Demons often leaving their realm to visit this one. This land is considered a sacred space, where the living can commune with the dead. Some have devoted their lives to protect and maintain this place, hence the large amount of Knights and Workers present.

Hierarchy

The Undead Temple is ruled by Grimora, a powerful Scrybe who has mastered the art of necromancy. Despite her somewhat fearsome appearance, she is known to be quite kind and caring towards her subjects, knowing each and every one of them by name.

By far her favorites though are the 4 Ghouls that rule over the 4 main areas of the temple: Kaycee Hobbes, Sawyer Patel, Cornelius Timberton and Royal Domingues. They are extremely loyal to Grimora, and do their best to keep the temple running.

Progression

The main goal is to reach Grimora's inner sanctum, locked inside the Crypt, where she resides. But the doors of the crypt are sealed, preventing anyone from entering. To open them, one must fill in the 4 missing parts of the epitaph carved on the door. Each part is held by one of Grimora's Ghouls, who will only give them up to those who prove their worth.

Every area has its own way of doing things, and its own unique challenges. But they all provide a way of summoning the Ghoul in charge of the area, usually by completing a special task or puzzle. Once summoned, the Ghoul will challenge the player to a duel, and if they win, they will receive their part of the epitaph.

Once all 4 parts are collected, the player can return to the Crypt and use them to unlock the door. Inside, they will find Grimora, who will challenge them to a final duel. If they win, they will have completed the Undead Temple.

Secret Route

Spoilers ahead! Proceed with caution.

One part that has not been mentionned yet is The Bone Lord, a mysterious figure who seems to have a deep connection to Grimora and the temple. He is said to be able to grant great power to those who serve him, but at a great cost. He is hidden beneath the Crypt, in a secret area that can only be accessed by those who know the way.

To reach him, the player must find hidden paths in all areas of the temple. These paths are often purposfully less interesting to follow, as a way to mislead the player. But should they try anyway, they will find a small shrine dedicated to the Bone Lord, where they can offer him a tribute in exchange for his favor.

If the player manages to find all 4 shrines and offer a tribute at each one, they will be granted access to the Bone Lord's lair beneath the Crypt. There, they will find him waiting, and he will tell them of some hidden truths about the game world, before putting them to a final test.

This battle is unlike any other in the game, not being a simple card battle and instead being entirely based on the chessboard map. If the player wins, the Bone Lord will sever a Horn from its skull and give it to the Player as an Inscrybing tool, as well as an extra Rare Card.
